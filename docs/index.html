Scattered platforms

Spreadsheet evaluations

Daily check-ups and quarterly reviews live in a spreadsheet, disconnected from the code activity that drives 80% of their content. Writing them means manually reconstructing what happened.

Nothing slips through

Every commit, PR, and MR across all your repos — GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Gitea — in one unified review queue. Age tracking shows what's been waiting. Multi-branch awareness means you see work across all branches, not just main.

Focus on what matters

AI-generated summaries tell you what changed before you open the diff. Risk levels flag the changes that need real attention — auth logic, database migrations, security-sensitive code. On-demand deep analysis reads your repo files for context-aware insights.

Evaluations write themselves

AI drafts daily check-ups and quarterly reviews from your accumulated review activity. You add the 20% that requires human observation — the conversations, the impressions, the context only you have. Stop reconstructing your team's work from git log.

How it works

1

Point to your repos

Add your local git repo paths. Optionally connect GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, or Gitea API tokens for PR/MR metadata.

2

See your queue

Vantage scans your repos and builds a unified review queue. AI categorizes and scores risk automatically.

3

Review and evaluate

Triage your queue, flag issues, write notes. When evaluation time comes, AI has already drafted it from your reviews.

Your data stays yours

Vantage runs entirely on your machine. Your code, evaluations, and review notes never leave your laptop. API tokens are encrypted at rest. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no accounts. Evaluations contain sensitive performance data — they belong on your machine, not someone else's server.

Get started in 60 seconds

git clone https://github.com/ThanhWilliamLe/vantage.git
cd vantage
pnpm install
pnpm dev
